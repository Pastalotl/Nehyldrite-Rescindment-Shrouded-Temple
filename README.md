# Nehyldrite: Rescindment
### Shrouded Temple

##### Made by Pastalotl
##### Music by Rufus Renty

## About this
Nehyldrite: Rescindment - Shrouded Temple is a small demo version of the larger concept of Nehyldrite: Rescindment. It is a fantasy themed turn-based RPG with sci-fi elements. The actual demo takes place entirely within the Shrouded Temple. You begin by selecting three characters from a total of seven, each of which are unique. You can then make your way through the temple to uncover its secrets.

## Limitations and Omitted Features
Unfortunately, this demo is currently at a fairly early stage. Many features work such as movement and the majority of actions, but many do not. The turn-based combat is current disabled since there are no organic triggers within the game. The enemies within the temple do not have any algorithms to deal damage or pursue you in any way. The main challenge of this demo is obstacles placed in doorways. There are a limited amount of assets and animations that have been made for the game and it is technically unfinished.  
Despite all this, I hope you can enjoy what is present in this demo, since it has taken me a fairly long time to produce. Feedback, comments and suggestions are welcome! This likely will not receive any changes to it for a while after I have posted it.
